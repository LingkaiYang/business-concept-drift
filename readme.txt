A synthetic dataset of 18 event logs based on Loan Assessment process, containing different types of gradual process drift.
Those datasets are generated based on Abderrahmane Maaradji's datasets [1]. The data can be downloaded from https://data.4tu.nl/articles/dataset/Business_Process_Drift/12712436


[1] A. Maaradji, M. Dumas, M. La Rosa, and A. Ostovar, “Detecting sudden and gradual drifts in business processes from execution traces,” IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering,
vol. 29, no. 10, pp. 2140–2154, 2017.
